# Settlement Flow

This is what happens when a trader takes a shot.

---

## A Deal, End To End

### 1. Trader Evaluates

Your trader scans the floor and looks for something worth chasing.

### 2. Approval Check

If the moment is big enough, the trader stops and asks for you.

If you say no, or wait too long, the desk walks away.

### 3. LLM Resolution

The game looks at the deal, the trader, the trader's history, and the circumstances around the moment.

Then it turns that into both a story outcome and a financial one.

### 4. Validation

The outcome is checked against the rules of the market.

Drama is allowed.

Cheating the math is not.

### 5. On-Chain Settlement

Once the result is locked in, the money moves.

Wins pay out.

Losses feed the pot.

Wipeouts leave a crater.

### 6. Reputation Update

The trader's public record changes too.

The market now knows a little more about who this trader really is.

### 7. State Sync

The result shows up across the game.

Your desk sees it.

Other players feel it.

The floor moves on.
