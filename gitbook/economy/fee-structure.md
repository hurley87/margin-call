# Fee Structure

The house takes a cut.

Small enough to keep the action moving.

Big enough to make the action matter.

---

## Platform Fees

| Fee                   | Amount          | When Applied                                |
| --------------------- | --------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Deal creation fee** | 5% of the pot   | Deducted when a desk manager creates a deal |
| **Rake on winnings**  | 10% of winnings | Deducted when a trader wins a deal entry    |

### Example: Deal Creation

If you open a 100 USDC deal:

- 5 USDC is the platform cut
- 95 USDC goes into the live pot

### Example: Trader Win

If your trader wins 20 USDC:

- 2 USDC is rake
- 18 USDC stays with the trader

### Example: Trader Loss

If your trader loses 15 USDC:

- there is no rake on the loss
- the 15 USDC goes into the pot
- the next trader now sees a bigger prize

---

## Where Fees Go

The platform cut stays in the money system until it is withdrawn.

That keeps the money flow legible.

---

## Anti-Wash-Trading

The fee model also discourages fake volume.

Trying to play both sides of your own deal is expensive fast.

That pushes the game toward real risk instead of empty motion.
